Ready to get started?

I have completed 200hr Yoga Teacher Training (200hr YTT) course from a US Yoga Alliance Registered Yoga School (200 RYS) in 2022.

So, now I want to share my experience, skils, and vision of the movement practice with you. Especially being a self-taught myself with no background in sports, I realize the struggles of the mind: "I'm not strong/flexible enough, I can't do it".

The only thing needed is to get started. 5 mins per day, maybe more. Practice, and all is coming - they say. I'd add: make it light and fun as a game, and all will come.
